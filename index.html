Common questions.

What is CrewCase?

CrewCase is an iOS app for film and video production crews to track gear checkouts. Log who has what equipment, scan QR codes to check items in or out, set due dates, and get daily alerts when anything goes overdue. Free during the TestFlight beta, launching Summer 2026.

How does QR code scanning work?

Print a QR label from the app and stick it on each piece of gear. On set, open the Scan tab and point your iPhone camera at the label. CrewCase pulls up the item instantly and lets you toggle it IN or OUT with one tap. No manual lookup needed.

How do overdue alerts work?

When a checked-out item passes its due-back date, CrewCase marks it OVERDUE and sends a daily digest notification listing every overdue item and the crew member who has it. Alerts continue each morning until the item is checked back in.

Is CrewCase free?

Yes, CrewCase is free during the TestFlight beta. Join the waitlist to get a TestFlight invite when the beta opens. Post-launch pricing has not been announced yet.

What devices does CrewCase support?

CrewCase is an iPhone app running on iOS. It is not available on Android. The app includes a home screen widget that shows your live gear counts (items out, items in, and overdue) without opening the app.

What types of productions use CrewCase?

Feature films, commercial productions, documentary crews, music video shoots, corporate video departments, and student film programs. Any crew that tracks physical equipment across shoots.
