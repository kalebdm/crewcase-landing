Common questions.

CrewCase is an iPhone app for tracking gear on film and video sets. Log who has what, scan QR codes to check items in or out, set due dates, and get daily alerts when anything goes overdue.

Print a QR label from the app and stick it on each piece of gear. On set, open the Scan tab and point your camera at the label. CrewCase pulls up the item and lets you toggle it IN or OUT with one tap. No typing required.

When a checked-out item passes its due-back date, CrewCase marks it OVERDUE and sends a daily notification with every overdue item and who has it. You'll keep getting that alert every morning until it's back.

iPhone only, no Android. There's also a home screen widget that shows your live gear counts without opening the app.

Feature films, commercial shoots, documentaries, music videos, corporate video, student film. If you track gear across shoots, it'll work for you.
