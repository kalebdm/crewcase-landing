Features

Everything your gear room needs.

From a single camera to a full truck of equipment — no spreadsheets, no sticky notes.

Check In / Check Out
Log who took what and when with a single tap. Assign any crew member as borrower and set a due-back date.
QR Code Scanning
Scan any item's QR label to instantly pull up its status and toggle IN/OUT. Print labels for every piece of gear.
Overdue Alerts
Daily digest notifications tell you exactly which items are overdue and who has them — before you have to go hunting.
Gear Kits
Bundle related items into named kits — Camera A Package, Sound Kit, Lighting Rig — and check them all out as one.
Crew Management
Maintain a roster of crew members and see each person's full borrowing history and what they currently have out.
Maintenance Mode
Flag gear that's in for repair. It shows a MAINT badge and is excluded from available inventory counts automatically.
Bulk Check-Out
Select multiple items and check them all out to the same crew member at once — perfect for fast-paced set days.
Reservations
Reserve gear for upcoming shoot dates. See what's available before your crew arrives and avoid double-booking.
Home Screen Widget
A glanceable widget shows your live out / in / overdue counts without opening the app.
How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

No setup fees, no server, no spreadsheets. Your gear inventory lives on your iPhone.

1
Import Your Inventory
Add items manually or import a CSV. Categories, IDs, photos, serial numbers, and purchase values all supported.
2
Print QR Labels
Generate QR codes for every item and print adhesive labels. Stick them on cases, cables, and bodies.
3
Add Your Crew
Add crew members once. From then on, checking gear out to anyone is a two-tap operation.
4
Scan and Go
On set, scan a QR code to check an item out. Scan again to check it back in. The activity log handles the rest.
